Favorite Band

Elani are a Kenyan pop group from Nairobi that consists of three members; Wambui Ngugi, Maureen Kunga and Brian Chweya. The trio of scholars met while still students at the Alliance Francaise, Nairobi and quickly becoming friends and since then their bond grew into realizing that they shared a common passion for music.

Their music is quite relatable, and in a language that is easy to understand, as it's about all that happens around us and to us. The kind of music that appeals to both the younger and older generations. They won the Best African Pop award at the 2014 All Africa Music Awards ceremony.

Maureen Kunga

Maureen Kunga is a singer,songwriter,guitarist and one of the two female vocalists with Elani and a lawyer who is creative, compassionate and a lover of life. She is the last born in a family of five. Her musical journey started from her appearances at the church choir where she was able to nurture her talent to the amazing woman and singer she is today.<

Born and bred in Nairobi, she obtained a degree in law from the University of Nairobi and got married in 2019.

Wambui Ngugi

Wambui Ngugi, the ever smiling beautiful Kenyan lady is a singer, songwriter and one of the female vocalists with Elani. She has a BSc in Actuarial Science from University of Nairobi and is very passionate in her christian belief. Wambui has an elder sister who she admires so much and one of her best hobby while she was growing up is swimming.
